I hope you are punching through your Getting Started Guide on your Dashboard. I wanted to check in and talk a little bit about Contacts.

Contacts are the foundation of your business, and of {{appname}}. {{appname}} is a contact-based system, which means that everything (Projects, Invoices, To-Dos) must all connect to a Contact.

What is a contact? Any person that is associated with your business, including:
 
  • Hot Leads ─ Hot Leads are potential customers. They are interested, but they have not paid you, or signed a contract with you.
  • Clients ─ Clients are your customers. These people have paid you, and/or signed a contract with you. 
  • Cold Leads ─ Cold Leads are people who were once interested (making them Hot Leads for a time), but they aren't as interested anymore. You want to keep their info, but you don't expect them to book any time soon. 
  • Other ─ Other contacts are just that. They are not leads or clients. Other contacts could be vendors, partners, or networking friends. They are associated with your business, but not a lead or client. 

You might be wondering: Why do I need to add all my Contacts? The fact is, creating your contact database is vital. While it might not seem to be now, I promise you that your future self will be grateful!

Keeping a database of Contacts with birthdays, email addresses, and phone numbers will come in handy throughout time. Plus, as your business grows, it's really rewarding to see just how many people you have met over time.
